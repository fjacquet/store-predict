### Non-Blocking Observation

`workload_category` is consumed by `health_checks.py` (lines 235, 254) but is not in `CANONICAL_COLUMNS`. It is added downstream by `classify_dataframe()` at upload time and persisted in session. This is a **pre-existing design pattern** (not introduced by v4.0 phases) and does not cause any regression. Tests handle it correctly via fixtures.
